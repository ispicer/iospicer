Curate Mag

Curate is a digital magazine meant to showcase different artists within the Philadelphia region. The zine consists of photos and interviews of 7 different Philadelphia-based artists. Interspersed within these interviews are pieces written by Isaiah that connect to some of the overall prevelant themes of the zine. The project was made with a combination of Photoshop, InDesign, and some web development. It is currently hosted at curatemag.com
